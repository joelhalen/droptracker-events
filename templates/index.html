DropTracker - Events

Event Styles

The DropTracker event system is host to numerous different types of events.
An italicized name denotes that the event is currently a work in progress.

Boss(es) of the Week

Compete to obtain the most points for the specified boss(es) on the given week! Points can be obtained by reaching milestone kill-counts, obtaining individual kills and receiving rare drops, to name a few methods.

Skill of the Week

The 'ol classic Skill of the Week, with some added twists courtesy our custom RuneLite plugin! Compete to obtain the most experience in the selected skill, obtain the most skill-related collection log slots/random events/clues, and much more!

Lost Lands

A uniquely-developed game custom-built for the DropTracker community, based loosely on the concepts presented in CandyLand, with Old School twists and a Mario-Kart style item system, where offensive and defensive items can help guide you to victory!

Bingo!

This one doesn't need a whole lot of explanation! It's the classic Old School Runescape Bingo! Compete alone or on a team against others to be the first to BLACKOUT the event bingo board, to receive 100% of the buy-ins/prize pool.
